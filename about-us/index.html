About Us

Source of inspiration for artists, friends of the most reputable fashion icons, girlfriends of the celebrities, who developed true passions for them, betrayal reasons or evidence of endless love, the diamonds have never lost the power of fascination.

Tradition

With 20 years of experience in the luxury jewelry market in Germany, 8 Jewellery Jewelry House recently opened their first store in Romania, in The Grand Avenue Gallery, along with the most famous international luxury brands.

8 Jewellery offers a selection of exquisite pieces, 18k gold, diamonds and gemstones, of some cult brands with tradition in the luxury jewelry industry, such as: Crivelli Gioielli, Damaso Martinez, but also its own collection of jewelry which is distinguished by a modern and elegant design.

The creations of the 8 Jewellery Jewelry House are distinguished primarily through a critical issue, namely that of being unique pieces. The jeweler artist had a different source of inspiration every time. From the mount, shape, innovative elements, such as the flexible handle for rings, to arranging the diamonds in a certain form, the 8 Jewellery jewelry can compete with the most prestigious jewelry houses in the world. 

Our store is addressed to all those who believe that a piece of jewelry is not a simple accessory or just an investment, but more than that. We believe that a jewel can become a signature bearing, encapsulated in it, an emotion, a moment of life, a memory, aspects that gives a unique value. This is why, we have created high quality lasting jewelry, diamond surfaced, known as symbols of eternity.

Refinement

8 Jewellery offers a selection of exquisite pieces, 18k gold, diamonds and gemstones, of some cult brands with tradition in the luxury jewelry industry, such as: Crivelli Gioielli, Damaso Martinez, but also its own collection of jewelry which is distinguished by a modern and elegant design.

The available designs from our store can satisfy even the most discerning tasted, be they traditional, classical or modern, primarily due to the diversity of models, from discrete, feminine jewelry, such as the fine Tenis bracelets in gradient with emeralds and diamonds, brilliant refined rings, the solitaire earrings, a completely atypical versatile creation, a piece of resistance, which can easily be transformed in a different pair of earrings only by removing one item, to statement, opulent, sculptural jewelry.

The yellow gold, bright, irradiates heat, the white one is restful and refined white, the black one is nonconformist. They perfectly merge in this sample of inventiveness and creativity, with the champagne diamonds, emeralds and sapphires.

Nothing is by chance, because, in turn, this outpouring of conceit is tempered by the sobriety of the decor. The effect is a true force of suggestion that mingle poetry, emotions, cultural influences, sophisticated taste and subtle play of shadows and glows.

The desire to provide a complete shopping experience to our customers can also be notice due to the attention paid in decorating the store, and due to the creative exposure set-up of the precious pieces.

Elegance

8 Jewellery own collection is distinguished by a modern and elegant design.

The concept of this precious space in the proper sense and figuratively is a refined intellectual approach that betrays concern for visual delight.

The design bears the signature of the artist Gunter Neunzig a renowned and apreciated interior designer in Germany whose creation, namely, the house in which he lives, managed to receive the recognition of specialists in the field along with its location in the top of the most original 10 houses in Europe .

The all-black decor combines perfectly with the 300 years old veneer mills which host the transparent jewelry boxes, a true art installation.

Thus, the sober black contrasts with the chameleonic brilliance of the jewelry and enhances their beauty.

These are the exigencies of an esthete, and the effect is added to the concerns of the stylist-jewelers regarding the visual impact of the creations.

The lighting exceeded its normal function, the objects being designed by an architect of light. The lamps are art objects because they induce emotions and intentions.

The luminaires with avant-garde forms of astral geometry and also the smoked type furniture have been chosen with great care, both belonging to the famous Italian brand MOOOI, which became iconic with the introduction of the pieces in the collections of the most prestigious museums: Victoria & Albert Museum, Groninger Museum sau Montreal Museum of Fine Arts.

8 Jewellery therefore proposed through this sophisticated mix in his sobriety, a unique experience that certainly will convince you that 8 Jewellery does not sell simple jewelry but EMOTIONS.
